2. Find out what the population and average life expectancy for people in Argentina (ARG) is. 

SELECT population FROM country WHERE name='Argentina';
ANS =  37032000


SELECT AVG(LifeExpectancy) FROM country WHERE name='Argentina';
ANS = 75.1
